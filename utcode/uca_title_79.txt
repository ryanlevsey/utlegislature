Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301 State energy policy.
---------------------------------------------------
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1): It is the policy of the state that:
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(a): Utah shall have adequate, reliable, affordable, sustainable, and clean energy resources;
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(b): Utah shall promote the development of:
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(b)(i): nonrenewable energy resources, including natural gas, coal, oil, oil shale, and oil sands;

Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(b)(iv): alternative transportation fuels and technologies;

Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(d)(iii): ensure the state's energy independence by promoting the use of energy resources generated within the state;
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(e): Utah shall allow market forces to drive prudent use of energy resources, although incentives and other methods may be used to ensure the state's optimal development and use of energy resources in the short- and long-term;
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(f): Utah shall pursue energy conservation, energy efficiency, and environmental quality;
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(g): Utah shall promote the development of a secure supply chain from resource extraction to energy production and consumption;
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(h):

Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(i): Utah shall maintain an environment that provides for stable consumer prices that are as low as possible while providing producers and suppliers a fair return on investment, recognizing that:
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(i)(i): economic prosperity is linked to the availability, reliability, and affordability of consumer energy supplies; and
Utah Code Annotated § 79-6-301(1)(i)(ii): investment will occur only when adequate financial returns can be realized;
